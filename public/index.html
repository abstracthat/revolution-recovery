A Sober Living Sanctuary for Healing in the Heart of the Mountains

At Revolution Recovery, we believe recovery is more than breaking free from addiction—it’s about rediscovering your whole self in a safe, supportive, and nurturing environment.

Nestled in the healing beauty of western North Carolina's Blue Ridge Mountains, our cozy Black Mountain cabin provides the perfect balance of peace, comfort, and community.

Here, the mountains themselves remind us of resilience, strength, and new beginnings. Surrounded by nature and supported by peers on the same journey, you’ll find the space to heal, grow, and rebuild a meaningful life.

Welcome to Revolution Recovery

A Supportive Recovery Community

Live alongside others who understand your journey. Shared bedrooms foster connection and accountability, while also creating a family-like atmosphere of encouragement.

Therapy & Personal Recovery Plans

For each resident we will create an individualized recovery plan. We provide therapy support if desired and guidance in connecting with trusted local resources for mental health, holistic healing, and long-term recovery support.

Healing Amenities

Enjoy access to our outdoor sauna and hot tub—spaces designed for relaxation, self-care, and renewal. Get outside and relax on nearby walking paths and hiking trails.

Convenient Location

Located just minutes from downtown Black Mountain and a short 20 minute drive to Asheville, our cabin combines natural tranquility with easy access to public transportation, shops, and a host of recovery-supportive community resources.

Your Cozy Mountain Home

Revolution Recovery isn’t just a program—it’s your home. Our warm and inviting mountain cabin provides:

  • Shared bedrooms with roommates for companionship and accountability
  • Comfortable, welcoming common spaces that foster connection and a place to relax
  • A peaceful retreat surrounded by fresh mountain air and nearby hiking trails
  • Two fully equipped kitchens and in-house laundry facilities
