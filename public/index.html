A Sober Living Home for Healing in WNC

At Revolution Recovery, we believe recovery is more than breaking free from addiction—it’s about rediscovering your whole self in a safe, supportive, and structured environment.

Nestled in the Blue Ridge Mountains of North Carolina, you’ll find the perfect balance of peace, accountability, and community support needed for long-term recovery.

Structure and Support for Recovery

  • Level II monitored sober living with a live-in house manager who is a Certified Peer Support Specialist.
  • Accountability and safety through regular and random drug screens that provide a buffer of support during vulnerable times.
  • Therapeutic foundations link residents with community providers for individual therapy and intensive outpatient programs.
  • Support in accessing transportation services and other community resources as needed.
  • Structured weekly house meetings provide both peer accountability and connection.

Therapy and Personal Recovery Plans

Each resident participates in creating a personalized recovery plan that acknowleges the importance of mental health throughout recovery. We facilitate connections with trusted community providers who offer SAIOP, individual and group therapy.

We encourage residents to take in the restorative beauty of nature—from nearby hiking trails to a quiet creek with a bench for peaceful prayer or meditation—while also engaging in wellness resources that support whole-person healing.

All plans are person-centered and respect the uniqueness of every recovery path, including residents on MAT (Medication Assisted Treatment).
