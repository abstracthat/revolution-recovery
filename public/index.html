A Sober Living Sanctuary for Healing in the Heart of the Mountains

At Revolution Recovery, we believe recovery is more than breaking free from addiction—it’s about rediscovering your whole self in a safe, supportive, and structured environment.

Nestled in the healing beauty of western North Carolina's Blue Ridge Mountains, our cozy Black Mountain cabin provides the perfect balance of peace, accountability, and community support needed for long-term recovery.

Here, the mountains remind us of resilience, strength, and new beginnings. Surrounded by nature and supported by peers on the same path, you’ll find the space to heal, grow, and rebuild a meaningful life.

Structure and Support for Recovery

To foster safety and growth, we combine the retreat like environment with stability of structured sober living.

  • Level II monitored sober living with a live-in house manager who is a Certified Peer Support Specialist, bringing both training and lived experience.
  • Accountability and safety through regular and random drug screens that provide a buffer of support during vulnerable times.
  • Therapeutic foundations provide facilitation in linking with community providers for individual therapy and intensive outpatient programs.
  • Transportation support to facilitate accessing transportation services and other community resources as needed.
  • Weekly house meetings are structured around a 12-step focus, offering both guidance and peer accountability.

Therapy and Personal Recovery Plans

Each resident participates in creating a personalized recovery plan. We facilitate connections with trusted community providers who offer SAIOP (Substance Abuse Intensive Outpatient Programs), individual therapy, and other recovery-focused services.

We encourage residents to take in the restorative beauty of nature—from nearby hiking trails to a quiet creek with a bench for peaceful prayer or meditation—while also engaging in wellness resources that support whole-person healing.

All plans are person-centered and respect the uniqueness of every recovery path, including support for residents on MAT.
